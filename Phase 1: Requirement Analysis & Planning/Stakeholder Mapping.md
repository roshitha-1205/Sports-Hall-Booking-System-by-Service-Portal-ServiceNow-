# Stakeholder Mapping

## Description

This phase identifies the key stakeholders involved in the **Sports Hall Booking System** and defines their roles and responsibilities. Stakeholder mapping ensures that every participant understands their responsibilities, enabling smooth communication, efficient booking management, and successful system implementation.

---

## 👥 Stakeholder Mapping

| **Stakeholder** | **Responsibility** |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| **End Users** | Book sports halls, view availability, manage reservations, track booking status, and cancel bookings when required. |
| **Sports Hall Managers** | Review, approve or reject booking requests, manage sports hall availability, and oversee facility scheduling. |
| **Facility Administration** | Monitor facility utilization, maintain sports hall records, generate reports, manage maintenance schedules, and ensure efficient operation of the booking system. |

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify all stakeholders involved in the booking process.
- Clearly define stakeholder roles and responsibilities.
- Improve communication between users, managers, and administrators.
- Ensure efficient management of sports facilities and booking workflows.

---

## ✅ Expected Outcome

A well-defined stakeholder structure that enables smooth coordination, transparent booking approvals, efficient facility management, and improved user experience throughout the Sports Hall Booking System.
