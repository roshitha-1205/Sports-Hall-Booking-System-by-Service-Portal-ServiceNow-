# 📋 Phase 1 – Requirement Analysis & Planning

## 📖 Description

In this phase, the business requirements for the **Sports Hall Booking System** were gathered and analyzed. The existing manual booking process was studied to identify challenges and opportunities for automation. Key functionalities, user roles, booking workflows, and approval processes were defined to establish a clear understanding of the system requirements.

A structured implementation plan and project timeline were also prepared to guide the development process, ensuring that each phase of the project could be executed efficiently and within the planned schedule.


## 📌 Objectives

- Gather and analyze business requirements.
- Identify key stakeholders and user roles.
- Define functional and non-functional requirements.
- Design the booking and approval workflow.
- Prepare the project implementation plan.
- Establish the project development timeline.

---

## 📂 Deliverables

- Business Objectives
- Functional Scope
- Stakeholder Mapping
- Execution Roadmap

---

## 📋 Included Tasks

### ✅ Business Objectives

- Digitize the sports hall booking process.
- Improve user convenience through an online booking portal.
- Automate booking approvals and notifications.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Enable centralized reporting and analytics.
- Promote resource optimization and sustainability.

---

### ✅ Functional Scope

- Service Portal Widgets and Pages
- Real-time Slot Availability
- Booking Validation
- Approval Workflow
- Email Notifications
- Payment & Refund Handling *(Future Enhancement)*

---

### ✅ Stakeholder Mapping

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|-------------|----------------|
| End Users | Book sports halls and manage reservations |
| Sports Hall Managers | Approve bookings and manage availability |
| Facility Administration | Monitor utilization, maintenance, and reports |

### ✅ Execution Roadmap
