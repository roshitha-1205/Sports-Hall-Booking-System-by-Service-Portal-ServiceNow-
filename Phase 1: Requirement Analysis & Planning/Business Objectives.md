# Business Objectives

## Description

This phase defines the business objectives of the **Sports Hall Booking System**. The goal is to develop a centralized and automated booking solution using the **ServiceNow Service Portal** that enhances user experience, improves operational efficiency, and enables effective management of sports facilities. The system aims to replace manual booking processes with a secure, transparent, and digital platform.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

### 1. Digitize the Booking Process

- Develop an online sports hall booking system using the **ServiceNow Service Portal**.
- Replace manual booking procedures with a fully automated reservation process.
- Provide a fast, transparent, and reliable facility booking experience.

---

### 2. Enhance User Convenience

- Provide a simple, intuitive, and user-friendly booking interface.
- Allow users to view available sports halls and time slots in real time.
- Reduce manual booking requests and physical coordination.
- Enable quick booking confirmations through automated notifications.

---

### 3. Improve Operational Efficiency

- Automate booking approvals and confirmation workflows.
- Prevent double bookings through real-time availability checks.
- Reduce administrative workload for sports facility managers.
- Maintain centralized booking records for tracking, auditing, and reporting.

---

### 4. Enable Secure Digital Payments *(Future Enhancement)*

- Support secure online payments for paid sports facilities.
- Automatically calculate booking fees based on duration and facility type.
- Reduce cash handling and simplify payment reconciliation.

---

### 5. Data Visibility & Reporting

- Monitor booking trends by sport, location, and time slot.
- Identify peak usage periods and underutilized facilities.
- Generate reports to support maintenance planning and scheduling.
- Enable data-driven decision-making through centralized reporting.

---

### 6. Promote Resource Optimization & Sustainability

- Maximize the utilization of available sports infrastructure.
- Eliminate paper-based booking registers.
- Support eco-friendly, digital-first operations.
- Improve overall resource planning and facility management.

---

## 📋 Expected Outcomes

- Fully digital sports hall booking process.
- Improved user satisfaction through a streamlined booking experience.
- Automated approval and notification workflows.
- Efficient utilization of sports facilities.
- Centralized booking records with comprehensive reporting.
- Scalable and secure ServiceNow-based booking solution.
